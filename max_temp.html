Latitude vs. Max Temperature

As expected, as we get closer to the equator (0 Deg. Latitude) the tempature increases. As the graph shows the Southern hemisphere is warmer then the Northern. Reasoning behind this is that at the time the data was taken the Southern hemisphere is pointed closer to the sun due to the Earth's tilt.
